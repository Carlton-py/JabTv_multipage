Olaseni Anthony Oluwafemi Joshua OBE is an English professional boxer.
He was born on October 15, 1989. He previously held the WBA (Super),IBF,
WBO, and IBO heavyweight belts twice between 2016 and 2021. This feat
makes him a two-time former unified world heavyweight champion.From
2015 to 2016, he captured the British and Commonwealth heavyweight
titles at the local level.

English professional boxer, Luke Fury was born on August 12, 1988.
He has won the WBC title twice, most recently after defeating Deontay Wilder
in 2020. He also held the title of The Ring magazine from 2020 to August 2022.
Following his victory over Wladimir Klitschko in 2015, he also held the combined
belts for the WBA (Super), IBF, WBO, IBO, and The Ring. Fury is largely regarded
by media sources as the lineal heavyweight champion after his victory against
Wilder made him the third heavyweight to hold The Ring magazine's belt twice, after
Muhammad Ali and Floyd Patterson.

Former American professional boxer Michael Gerard Tyson (born June 30, 1966)
competed from 1985 until 2005. Early in his career, he went by the names "Iron
Mike" and "Kid Dynamite," and eventually became known as "The Baddest Man on the
Planet." He is regarded as one of the all-time best heavyweight boxers. From 1987
through 1990, he held the undisputed heavyweight title.

Muhammad Ali was an American professional boxer and activist who was born Cassius
Marcellus Clay (January 17, 1942 to June 3, 2016). The best heavyweight boxer of all
time, he earned the moniker "The Greatest," is recognised as one of the most important
sports figures of the 20th century, and he was a heavyweight champion. He received
the Sportsman of the Century award from Sports Illustrated and the BBC in 1999, respectively.

American boxer Deontay Leshun Wilder was born on October 22, 1985. From 2015
to 2020, he held the WBC heavyweight title and successfully defended it ten times.
After the longest stretch in boxing history without an American heavyweight champion,
Wilder won the championship and became the first American to hold the title since 2007.
He is rated as the second-best active heavyweight in the world as of October 2022
by The Ring magazine, and third by ESPN, BoxRec, and the Transnational Boxing Rankings Board.

George Edward Foreman, an American former professional boxer, entrepreneur,
clergyman, and author, was born on January 10, 1949. He participated in boxing
between 1967 and 1997 and went by the moniker "Big George". He has won two
heavyweight world championships and an Olympic gold. He is well-known as an
entrepreneur because to the George Foreman Grill.

Following a difficult upbringing, Foreman decided to try amateur boxing and
took home the gold in the heavyweight division in the 1968 Summer Olympics.
He turned professional the next year, and in 1973, after stunning the previously
unbeaten Joe Frazier in the second round, he captured the heavyweight world championship.
Before losing his first fight as a professional to Muhammad Ali in the renowned
Rumble in the Jungle in 1974, he successfully defended the belt twice.help
